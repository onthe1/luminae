About
Luminae was built on a simple truth. Physical media doesn't last forever, but your legacy should.
Photos fade. Film degrades. Hard drives fail. Camera cards corrupt. And with them, the moments that matter most can disappear forever.
With 25 years of experience in television, digital media and high-end client tech services, we understand what it means to handle irreplaceable things with care. Our clients expect nothing less than the best.
No middlemen. No shortcuts. Just a trusted team dedicated to preserving your story.
